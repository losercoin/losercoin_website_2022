Losercoin Story

Losercoin (ticker symbol: $LOWB; BSC contract: 0x843D4a358471547f51534e3e51fae91cb4Dc3F28, ETH contract: 0x69e5C11a7C30f0bf84A9faECBd5161AA7a94decA) is a project initiated by two poor guys from China, one is a father of two kids from a village, the other one is a coder living a miserable life by working 60 hours a week. Two founders first heard about Bitcoin in 2017 and lost a shit ton of money trading it. They started LOWB project as an open journey and were fully prepared to lose everything. The two founders have added their whole life savings into the liquidity pool on the first day. It is guaranteed that the project team will not manipulate the price of $LOWB due to lack of capital, and there will be no rug pull.
